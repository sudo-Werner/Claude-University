EXERCISE

A weight w has gradient ∂L/∂w = 0.4.

With learning rate η = 0.1, write the gradient-descent update for w.

Gradient descent moves against the gradient: w ← w − η · ∂L/∂w
SOLUTION
w ← w − (0.1 × 0.4) = w − 0.04
Each step subtracts the learning rate times the gradient — a small move downhill on the loss.
